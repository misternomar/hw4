Software Development Life Cycle



Planning


The planning phase requires a confluence of product and project management. The main point of planning is to be able to successfully deliver a software product that meets everyone's needs. During this phase, the allocation of resources have to be determined. The capacity and scope of the project have to be delineated. A schedulue has to be designed to meet deadlines. There has to be a determination and projection in cost. Provisions have to be defined.
